✏️ Zone Editor

Calibrate the zone polygons to match your yard exactly.

How to edit a zone:
1. Pick a zone below and click Load
2. Click on the map to add / move points
3. Right-click to remove the last point
4. Enter or Save when done
5. Esc to clear in-progress points
6. F to reset view, E to toggle panel
